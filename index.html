About Us
Rustic Mill Shop

Rustic Mill Bakery, also used to be known as Saamaria's Continental Bakery, specializes in Traditional Italian Breads, Sourdoughs, Focaccias, Smallgoods, Olives, Preserves and a wide variety of Italian grocery products. We are not only about bread and pastries, but we also offer fresh cheeses, hams, prosciutto, sauces, olives, pasta, jams and many more. We dedicate ourselves to provide the best experience-for our customers-in every bite.
Always Fresh

We promise to uphold a quality that provides products that are always fresh.
Mouthwatering Taste

We are dedicated to make every product we make the tastiest to provide mouthwatering experience
Made with best ingredients

In Rustic Mill, to ensure the freshness and taste of our products, we choose and use the best ingredients we can.
Baked with Love

Most importantly, we enjoy and we love what we do, and we hope to share this love and joy with you.
